My Final Project

For my final project, my partner, Aiden Cox, and I decided to make a game
that resembled Space Invaders. So the way we created the game is by getting inspiration from
how we had created from a game called Frogger in CSC106, where a frog was dto cross the road of
moving cars without dying. We grasped this same concept, but instead of cars going vertically
we had rocks go horizontally.

To make the game more interesting, we made the player die once they lost three lives.
Each time a player hits a rock, they lose one life. However, to add some more creativness to the game
we also added random hearts that will give the player an extra life. The player has three characters to
choose from, either my bitmoji, the bitmoji of Aiden Cox, or the notorious Oh Noes guy in the Kkhan
Academy programming environment.
